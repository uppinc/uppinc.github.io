Android App Icon Instruction (Including Adaptive Icon)

This website helps the developer to resize and create your own Android App Icon, including legacy and adaptive app icon. You can simply upload your own design picture in the foreground and choose your icon's background color.

 

Adaptive Icon Instruction

AndroidManifest.xml 

Add the following code in your AndroidManifest.xml within the app folder.

<application
    …
    android:icon="@mipmap/ic_launcher"
    android:roundIcon="@mipmap/ic_launcher_round"
    …>
</application>

<adaptive-icon xmlns:android="http://schemas.android.com/apk/res/android">
    <background android:drawable="@color/ic_launcher_background" />
    <foreground android:drawable="@mipmap/ic_launcher_foreground" />
</adaptive-icon>
